01

Battery State-of-Health

Imperial College London · Faraday Institution · ongoing publication

A novel non-invasive method to recover a lithium-ion cell’s degradation modes straight from its open-circuit-voltage curves — without ever opening the cell. I replaced a local-minimum scipy fit with a Differential-Evolution global fit (0.0004 vs 0.005 least-squares), then built DV_fit — differential-voltage feature-matching via optimised Dynamic Time Warping — and Degraded_OCV_fit over a Pchip-DTW degradation manifold. NMC811–graphite, 41 cells.
